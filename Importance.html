Algorithms in our life

Generally, algorithms are present everywhere in our everyday life and take several different forms

Some examples of these algorithms are the following:

  • Traffic lights at roads
  • Searching a book in a library
  • Tying shoelaces
  • etc.

Algorithms in computer science

Algorithms are used in every part of computer science. They play a very important role in this field as they constitute the basis on which computers we use today have been built.

In addition to the functionality of computers and their use in problem-solving, algorithms give us the most ideal option for accomplishing a task. More specifically, they can be used to improve the efficiency of a computer programme, whether concerning accuracy or speed and they contribute to the proper utilization of resources (computer memory).
