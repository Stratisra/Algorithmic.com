What is an algorithm?

An algorithm is a procedure used for solving a problem or performing a computation. Algorithms act as a list of instructions that conduct specified actions step by step in either hardware or software-based routines.

For example, following a recipe is a great example of an algorithm which is applicable in everyday life. It comprises a replicable set of specific steps to accomplish a predetermined goal (such as cooking spaghetti or baking a cake).
